Likhita's Family

Welcome to Likhita's Cozy Cafe, where we believe in brewing connections and serving quality coffee. Founded in 2k20, our journey began with a passion for coffee and a vision to create a welcoming space for the community. Over the years, we have sourced the finest beans from sustainable farms around the world, ensuring that every cup is crafted with care and expertise. Our menu features not only exceptional coffee but also a delightful selection of breakfast items and pastries, all made with fresh ingredients. At Likhita's, we invite you to relax, connect, and enjoy the rich flavors of our offerings in a cozy atmosphere that feels like home.
